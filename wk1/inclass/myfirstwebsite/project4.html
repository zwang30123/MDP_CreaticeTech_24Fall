Lyric Oracle

A Machine’s Take on Your Life’s Questions

Concept

Lyric Oracle bridges life’s big (or small) questions with the randomness of machine-generated responses. It’s a playful exploration where music, poetry, and algorithms collide to produce something unexpected and occasionally profound. What if a song lyric could truly answer your question? And what happens when two favorite songs combine into a machine-created poem? Let’s find out.

Keywords

API Integration, Lyric Generation, Music as Data, Algorithmic Poetry

What This Website Does

Ask & Answer: Input any question, pick a music genre, and get a random lyric that speaks to your query. Poetry Mashups: Combine lyrics from a random song with a user-provided favorite song to generate a machine-crafted six-line poem. Listen Instantly: Links to Spotify and YouTube ensure you can dive straight into the music that inspired your answers.

How It Works

Question Meets Music:

— Input a question and select a genre.

— The system fetches a lyric line from a random song in the chosen genre to provide an answer.

Song Mashup Poetry:

— Input a favorite song, and the system combines its lyrics with another song’s lyrics to craft a unique, short poem.

Fallback Plan:

— If lyrics are unavailable, motivational messages step in with personalized guidance, because sometimes, the best advice is already within you.

Key Fun Features

Randomized Responses:

Each answer starts with one of three unique introductory styles, keeping things fresh and dynamic.

Machine-Crafted Poetry:

The system blends two sets of lyrics into a quirky yet meaningful poem, showcasing the playful potential of machine creativity.

Interactive Design:

Hover animations, scrollable results, and clean aesthetics enhance user engagement and experience.

Technical Overview

APIs in Action:

— Spotify API: Fetches songs by genre.

— Lyrics.ovh API: Retrieves song lyrics.

— YouTube API: Links relevant music videos.

Core Technologies:

— JavaScript drives the dynamic content generation and API calls.

— CSS ensures responsive design with scrollable output areas and fun hover effects.

Challenges Tackled:

— Missing lyrics are gracefully handled with fallback messages to maintain functionality.

— Poetry generation is capped at six lines to ensure brevity and coherence.

Fun Examples

Reflection

Effort & Challenges:

This project combines music, randomness, and user interactivity into a seamless experience. Generating poetry from shuffled lyrics highlights the creative potential of machines, even when the results feel chaotic. Handling missing lyrics and ensuring poems remained short yet coherent were key challenges tackled successfully.

What Could Be Improved:

Expanding the genre and song selection or adding mood-based lyric generation could enhance personalization and make the tool feel even more dynamic.

Final Thoughts:

Lyric Oracle turns simple inputs into creative outputs, sparking joy, laughter, and even a bit of reflection. It’s a lighthearted exploration of how machines reinterpret human creativity, offering users a playful lens to view music and language.
