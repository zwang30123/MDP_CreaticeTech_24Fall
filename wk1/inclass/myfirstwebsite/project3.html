Synthetic Relationships


SymbioSense

By Zita Wang & Jane Lu

Concept

In this project, we explore how plants in nature can communicate by creating a speculative system where two electronic beings mimic this exchange of information. This project reimagines plant communication in a digital context, emphasizing the symbiotic relationship between nature and technology.

Keywords

Digital Plant Communication, Symbiotic Interaction, Sensor-Driven Response, Environmental Feedback Loop, Nature-Inspired Automation, Adafruit IO, ESP32 Feather, Arduino IDE

Idea Diagram

Drawing inspiration from the interconnectedness of plant ecosystems, one being, designed as a mushroom provide humidity data. The second being, a mechanical flower, responds to this data by opening or closing. The feedback loop is completed when the mechanical flower sends signals back to the mushroom thorugh photo resistor, which lights up to represent the ongoing interaction.
